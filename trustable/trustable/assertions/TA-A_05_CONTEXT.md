**Guidance**

This assertion is satisfied to the extent that the DrivaPi safety case (arguments, evidence) is reviewed and updated on changes or quarterly, ensuring ongoing confidence.

This includes aggregating evidence from tenets/assertions, with breaks for evolving components like AI models.

**Evidence**

- Safety case document with:
  - Update history and dates
  - Reviewers
- Links to all evidence (e.g., artifacts/)

**Confidence scoring**

CS-A_05 is based on update frequency and coverage of confidence measures.

**Checklist**

- Could the case miss some evidence (e.g., new hazards)?
- Could there be outdated sections?
- Does it cover all tenets?
- Are updates triggered by changes?
