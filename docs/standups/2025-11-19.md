## Standards & Research

**TSF Framework Redesign:**
- Defining TSF tenets (core principles)
- Establishing trustable assertions methodology
- New model structure:
  - **Expectations:** What the system should do
  - **Assertions:** Claims about system behavior
  - **Evidence:** Proof that assertions are true
- Moving away from rigid V-model layers (URD/SRD/SWD/LLTC)
- Goal: More flexible and assertion-based traceability framework
